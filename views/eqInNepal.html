Earthquake In Nepal
Link to GitHub:   https://premdhakal.github.io/htmlFinalProject/

About Project:

7.8 magnitude of earthquake hit Nepal in April, 2015. Which left nearly 10,000 people dead and many more injured. The goal of this project is to created a site where visitor/volunteer can use important information like current development and lookup emergency contact numbers. Later, donate functionlity was added to the site inorder to collect donation globally. For that purpose function like currency conventor was also later implemented.

Technology Used Features Implemented
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Dreamweaver
  • HTML5
  • CSS
  • notepad++
  • Current News
  • Donation form
  • Currency Conventor
  • Emergency Contacts
  • Images/Videos++

Home Page: Simple and elegant home page consisting of logo, navigation bar, sections, paragraphs, forms and outside links. Here, users can get the new developments about the earthquake and aftermath.

Emergency Page: Here, users can get important infromation about emergency numbers to dial while in Nepal.

Donate Page: Main donate page which includes donation form and extra information about where and how to donate.

Currency Page: Here, users can get convert their currency before sending it to so they know how much it would be in Nepali Rupees.

Images Page: Here, users can get view images of earthquake.

Videos Page: Live footages and recorded footages of the earthquake.
